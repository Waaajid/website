about us

Since 1951, Yelloe has been synonymous with quality mangoes. With a rich heritage and years of expertise, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the market. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every mango we deliver is of the highest quality, packed with natural sweetness and juiciness. At Yelloe, customer satisfaction is paramount. We take pride in hand-selecting the finest mangoes, cultivated with care and expertise. Our team of dedicated farmers and experts work tirelessly to bring you the best mangoes, ensuring that each bite is a delightful experience. With Yelloe, you can trust that you are receiving mangoes that have been nurtured to perfection. Our passion for delivering exceptional mangoes remains unwavering as we continue to uphold our legacy of providing top-notch products to our valued customers. Join us on this flavorful journey and indulge in the unparalleled taste of Yelloe mangoes. Experience the difference and savor the sweetness that only our quality mangoes can offer.
